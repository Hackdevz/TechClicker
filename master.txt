Upgrades

	*fixBug
        name:Fix the bug
        desc:Fix a bug with <b>10% of your</b> money. ( It works only 90% of the time)
        no buy
        on click:if (money>=(money/10)) yield -(money/10) money
        on click:
            if (money>=(money/10))
                if (chance(90%))
                    yield 1 bugsFix
		    yield (data/10) data
                    toast You fixed the bug!
                else
                    toast Bip bup, the bug is still there 
                end
            end
        end
